🎓 Get Hired Without Formal School
The AI industry rewards demonstrable skill over credentials. Here's the real playbook.
1. Build in public — your portfolio IS your degree
Post projects on GitHub, Behance, X, or YouTube. Recruiters Google you. 3–5 real projects beats any certificate.
2. Get specific fast — pick one lane
"I build AI content workflows for e-commerce brands" beats "I work with AI." Specific always wins.
3. Start with freelance gigs
Upwork, Contra, Scale AI, Outlier — all hire without degrees. Take 2–3 paid gigs, collect testimonials, then pitch full-time.
4. Use free certs strategically
Google, DeepLearning.AI, Coursera offer free/cheap certs. Pair one cert with one project on your LinkedIn. They signal seriousness.
5. Network where the jobs actually are
Most AI jobs are filled before they're posted. Join Discord communities, attend Luma events, DM builders on X. One real relationship > 100 applications.
6. Post a "hire me" post on LinkedIn
Describe your skills, what you want, and link your portfolio. Share it on relevant communities. People land full-time roles this way in under 2 weeks.
